General Advice:

  • Stay organised by keeping all your notes in one place. This way, you won't be scrambling to find important notes the day before your A-level exam!
  • Spend Year 12 making reusable revision resources (e.g cue cards). It makes Year 13 so much easier. These are best done on a computer and backed up online.
